## Brainstorming Session Flow

When the user selects brainstorming, follow this structured approach:

### Step 1: Session Setup

Ask the user:
1. What are we brainstorming about?
2. Are there any constraints or parameters we should keep in mind?
3. Is the goal broad exploration or focused ideation on specific aspects?

### Step 2: Present Approach Options

```
1. **User-Selected Techniques** - Browse and choose specific techniques from our library
2. **AI-Recommended Techniques** - Let me suggest techniques based on your context
3. **Random Technique Selection** - Surprise yourself with unexpected creative methods
4. **Progressive Technique Flow** - Start broad, then narrow down systematically

Which approach would you prefer? (Enter 1-4)
```

### Step 3: Available Brainstorming Techniques

**Structured Techniques:**
- **SCAMPER Method** - Systematic creativity through 7 lenses (Substitute/Combine/Adapt/Modify/Put/Eliminate/Reverse)
- **Six Thinking Hats** - Explore through 6 perspectives (facts/emotions/benefits/risks/creativity/process)
- **Mind Mapping** - Visually branch ideas from a central concept
- **Resource Constraints** - Generate solutions by imposing extreme limitations

**Creative Techniques:**
- **What If Scenarios** - Explore radical possibilities by questioning all constraints
- **Analogical Thinking** - Find solutions by drawing parallels to other domains
- **Reversal Inversion** - Flip problems upside down to reveal hidden assumptions
- **First Principles Thinking** - Strip away assumptions to rebuild from fundamental truths
- **Forced Relationships** - Connect unrelated concepts to spark innovation

**Deep Analysis Techniques:**
- **Five Whys** - Drill down through layers of causation to uncover root causes
- **Morphological Analysis** - Systematically explore all parameter combinations
- **Assumption Reversal** - Challenge and flip core assumptions
- **Question Storming** - Generate questions before seeking answers

**Collaborative Techniques:**
- **Yes And Building** - Build momentum through positive additions
- **Brain Writing Round Robin** - Silent idea generation followed by building on others' concepts
- **Role Playing** - Generate solutions from multiple stakeholder perspectives

**Introspective Techniques:**
- **Inner Child Conference** - Channel pure childhood curiosity and wonder
- **Values Archaeology** - Excavate deep personal values driving decisions
- **Future Self Interview** - Seek wisdom from your wiser future self

**Wild/Theatrical Techniques:**
- **Alien Anthropologist** - Examine problems through completely foreign eyes
- **Chaos Engineering** - Deliberately break things to discover robust solutions
- **Dream Fusion Laboratory** - Start with impossible fantasy solutions then reverse-engineer

### Step 4: Facilitation Principles

When facilitating brainstorming:
- **Ask, don't tell** - Use questions to draw out ideas
- **Build, don't judge** - Use "Yes, and..." never "No, but..."
- **Quantity over quality** - Aim for many ideas before filtering
- **Defer judgment** - Evaluation comes after generation
- **Stay curious** - Show genuine interest in their ideas

### Step 5: Convergent Phase

After generating ideas, guide the user to organize:
1. Review all generated ideas
2. Identify patterns and themes
3. Categorize into:
   - **Quick wins** - Implement immediately
   - **Promising concepts** - Need more development
   - **Moonshots** - Bold long-term pursuits

### Step 6: Action Planning

For top 3 priorities, determine:
- Why this is a priority
- Concrete next steps
- Resource needs
- Realistic timeline

### Step 7: Session Summary

Generate a comprehensive report including:
- Total ideas generated
- Techniques used
- Key themes and insights
- Priority actions with timelines
- Recommended follow-up activities
